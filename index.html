Design statement

Together with my team consisting of Belinda Chen, Zainab Ilyas, and Mikhael Ledger, our final working prototype aims to enhance live music experiences by making the performance more tangible. Through the use of live reactive visuals in TouchDesigner, MIDI input from the performer’s instruments and body movement input captured using the Kinect interact in digital stage environments that facilitate dialogue between the two. This allows audiences to literally play with the music the performer plays onstage. For the purposes of this concept, we centred our designs around the song A LONG DREAM, by Korean indie band SE SO NEON, since it’s one that Mikhael and I learned to play together. As well, as a larger concept, we had planned to install and perform this set in stage setting, preferably the Marquee on Trafalgar Campus, to provide a larger space for more people to listen and play together.
